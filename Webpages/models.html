3D Models Info

Here you can find information about the 3D models used in our AR demos.

  • Rhinoceros:

    • Rhinoceroses are large, herbivorous mammals identified by their characteristic horned snouts.

  • Forest Cat:

    • The forest cat is a wild feline found in various forests around the world.

  • Velociraptor:

    • Velociraptor is a genus of dromaeosaurid theropod dinosaurs that lived approximately 75 to 71 million years ago during the later part of the Cretaceous Period.

  • Dolphin Fossil:

    • The dolphin fossil reveals that dolphins have been evolving for over 50 million years, with ancient species showcasing unique adaptations like long snouts and specialized teeth.
